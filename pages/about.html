About Us | Mountain Explorer | Discover, Explore and Connect.

Our Mission

At Mountain Explorer, our mission is to inspire and enable outdoor enthusiasts to explore the breathtaking mountain parks of Alberta and British Columbia. We aim to provide comprehensive, up-to-date, and user-friendly information about hiking trails, outdoor activities, accommodations, and more.

Our Team

Our team is composed of passionate hikers, seasoned travelers, and dedicated professionals who share a love for the great outdoors. We work tirelessly to ensure that our users have the best possible experience when planning their adventures.

Our Partnerships

We are proud to collaborate with a variety of external organizations, including local tourism operators, ski resorts, and government agencies like Parks Canada. These partnerships allow us to provide our users with the most accurate and relevant information.

Get Involved

We believe in the power of community and encourage our users to contribute to our website. Whether it’s sharing your hiking experiences, posting photos of your adventures, or providing feedback on our content, your contributions help make Mountain Explorer a valuable resource for all outdoor enthusiasts.

We also welcome support in various forms. If you’re interested in sponsoring our website or collaborating with us, please get in touch.
