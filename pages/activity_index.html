Activities in the Bow Valley

Explore exciting activities in the Bow Valley region.

Local Ski Areas

Experience the thrill of skiing in the heart of the Canadian Rockies. Check out these popular ski areas:

  • Sunshine Village: Experience world-class skiing and snowboarding at Sunshine Village, located in the heart of the Canadian Rockies.
  • Lake Louise: Discover breathtaking scenery and unparalleled skiing opportunities at Lake Louise Ski Resort.
  • Norquay: Enjoy stunning views and diverse terrain for all skill levels at Norquay Ski Resort.

Free Activities

Explore the beauty of the Bow Valley with these free activities:

  • Hike: Discover a variety of hiking trails for all skill levels.
  • Mountain Climb: Challenge yourself with a mountain climbing adventure.
  • Scenic Drive: Enjoy the stunning landscapes of the Bow Valley on a scenic drive.
  • Parks and Picnic Areas: Relax in one of the many parks and picnic areas.
  • Nature Walks: Take a leisurely walk and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
  • Wildlife Viewing: Spot local wildlife in their natural habitat.
  • Outdoor Markets: Browse local products and produce at outdoor markets.
  • Cultural Events: Experience local culture at various events and festivals.

Paid Activities

For a bit of extra fun, check out these paid activities:

  • Hot Springs: Relax and rejuvenate in natural hot springs.
  • Snow Tubing: Enjoy a thrilling ride down the slopes on a snow tube.
  • Axe Throwing: Try your hand at this unique and exciting activity.
  • Guided Tours: Learn more about the area with a guided tour.
  • Adventure Excursions: Get your adrenaline pumping with adventure excursions.
  • Workshops and Classes: Learn a new skill or hobby at local workshops and classes.
  • Equipment Rentals: Rent equipment for your outdoor adventures.
  • Attractions and Landmarks: Visit local attractions and landmarks.
